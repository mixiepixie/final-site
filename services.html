Services

Consider our fully staffed and serviced reception hall for your special day. We decorate for you and cleanup afterwards so that you are left with no hassle on your wedding day. You can bring in your own decorations and catering if you would like to do so.

After a dinner from our hotel restaurant, feel free to roam around our extensive gardens just outside the reception hall doors. We offer decorations and catering outside as well.

Our Hotel offers a range of suites for full-time missionaries. Our first option, for married couples, is the Super Deluxe Missionary Suite. For single missionaries on full-time missions, we offer the smaller Double Queen Queen Suite. These luxurious accomodations are designed to make your stay as comfortable as possible.

Enjoy our Summer Youth activites on our Grounds this Summer! Feel free to use the grounds for activites for church youth, young woman, and young men groups.
